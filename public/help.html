Forum Dog — How it works

TL;DR: Register a username, post threads, reply, attach images. That's it. No email, no password, no BS.

Getting started

  • First visit — you pick a username and optionally upload an avatar
  • Your session is saved in the browser forever. No login/logout, no passwords
  • Want a new account? Clear your cookies for this site and register again

Posting

  • New thread — go to any category, hit "New Thread", write title + content
  • Reply — scroll to the bottom of any thread, write your reply
  • Images — click "Attach Image" to upload PNG or JPG (up to 5MB). You'll see a preview before posting. Images show up inline in your post
  • You can attach multiple images to a single post

Your profile

  • Click your username in the header to see your profile
  • You can change your avatar anytime on your own profile page (click "Change Avatar")
  • Your post count and join date are shown publicly

Browsing

  • Main page shows all categories with their latest threads
  • Click a category name to see all threads in it
  • Thread icons: 📄 = normal, 💬 = has replies, 📌 = pinned, 🔒 = locked
  • Bottom of the page shows who's currently online

Admin stuff

  • Go to /admin and enter the admin password
  • Once you're admin, you can:
    • Create, edit, and delete categories
    • Change the forum name and description
    • Pin/unpin and lock/unlock threads
    • Delete any post or thread
    • Make other users admins
  • Admin controls appear directly on threads and posts — no need to go to the admin panel for moderation

Good to know

  • No email, no password recovery — if you clear cookies, that account is gone for you
  • Only PNG and JPG images are accepted
  • Max avatar size: 2MB (auto-resized to 150x150)
  • Max post image size: 5MB
